Our Legacy

A Dream Carried From Patiala

Sh. Mohan Lal Bloch arrived in Chandigarh with little more than faith, discipline, and a belief that honest work could build something lasting. He had left Patiala, Punjab as a young man with no safety net, carrying only the conviction that patient hands and a clean name could one day shape a better life for his family.

He opened his first furniture shop with borrowed tools and long working days, learning the grain of every wood and the temperament of every joint. Every customer who walked out of that shop carried more than furniture. They carried a piece made with care, craft, and the kind of integrity that slowly becomes reputation.

One shop became two. Two became five. Each new location was not just expansion; it was proof of decades spent protecting a standard. He built furniture the way he built his life: slowly, deliberately, and with the intention that it should last. Families returned across generations because they trusted the name behind the piece.

In 2021, Sh. Mohan Lal Bloch left this world, but his presence still guides the family, the workshops, and the people who worked beside him for years. The loss remains deeply felt. His chair may be empty, but his principles still sit at the head of every decision: respect the craft, respect the customer, and never compromise on what carries the Sunny Furniture name.

Today, his sons and grandsons carry that promise forward. Sunny Furniture is more than a business; it is a living tribute to one extraordinary life. Every sofa crafted, every dining table delivered, and every home that welcomes a piece from us continues the legacy of a man who proved that dreams, when worked for honestly enough, can find their way home.
